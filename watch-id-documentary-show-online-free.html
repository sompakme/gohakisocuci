Watch ID Documentary Show Online Free

The story of Ukrainian orphan Natalia Grace is once again back in the headlines with the Investigation Discovery (ID) series, The Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Natalia Speaks. A follow-up to 2023’s The Curious Case of Natalia Grace, the six-part series airs over three nights live on TV on the ID channel.

Tune in on Jan. 1, Jan. 2 and Jan. 3 to watch the Natalia Grace series on ID from 6-9 p.m. PT/9-11 p.m. ET. Don’t have cable? Here’s how to watch the Natalia Grace documentary online.

How to Watch Curious Case of Natalia Grace Documentary Online

The new season of the Natalia Grace docuseries is streaming exclusively on Max (formerly HBO Max) and Discovery+. A subscription to Max starts at just $9.99, while Discovery+ costs $4.99 a month, and both are available as add-on channels through DirecTV, Hulu and Prime Video.

Want to watch the Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Natalia Speaks for free? Amazon Prime members can test out Discovery+ with a seven-day free trial, which is more than enough time to stream all six episodes of the show for free.

How to Stream Curious Case of Natalia Grace Online Free

Another way to watch the Natalia Grace doc for free without cable? Sign up for this five-day free trial to DirecTV Stream, which lets you watch live TV on your phone, tablet, laptop or smart TV. Even better: DirecTV Stream carries Investigation Discovery as part of its channel lineup.

Other streaming options include Fubo (which has a seven-day free trial).

Can You Watch Natalia Grace Series on Amazon Prime?

Amazon currently has The Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Season 1 available to watch on-demand through Prime Video. You’ll have to purchase individual episodes for $2.99 or buy the full season (six episodes) for $9.79 here.

If you want to stream season two on Amazon, you’ll need to log in with your Amazon Prime membership and use this free trial to Discovery+.

Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Season 2 Episodes, Spoilers

The first season of The Curious Case of Natalia Grace captivated audiences with the story of little Natalia, a 6-year-old Ukrainian orphan diagnosed with a rare form of dwarfism who was adopted by an American family, the Barnetts. Everything appeared normal in their lives until the adoptive parents began to suspect that their “child” was actually a fully functioning adult masquerading as a little kid to terrorize them. And while court records and DNA testing seemingly confirmed that Natalia was a minor child born in 2003, both the Barnetts — and some viewers — remain unconvinced.

While The Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Season 1 focused primarily on Kristine and Michael Barnett, the second season, subtitled Natalia Speaks, will feature an “extensive, emotional sit-down between Natalia and adoptive father Michael Barnett” as they meet face-to-face for the first time since the shocking allegations of lies and abuse first went public. Per a press release, the two will “come to terms with the accusations that have been thrown around in both directions,” while offering new “insight into what really went on behind closed doors in the Barnetts’ home.”

The Curious Case of Natalia Grace: Natalia Speaks features six episodes that you can watch live Jan. 1-3 on ID, or stream on-demand through Discovery+. Each episode length is approximately 43 minutes, with two hourlong episodes (with commercials) airing each night.
