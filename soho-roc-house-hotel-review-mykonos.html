Soho Roc House Hotel Review, Mykonos

Uncluttered, airy and refreshingly simple, bedrooms combine organic, ethnically diverse and hedonistic touches — weathered wood minibars and stools, earthy kilim cushions, fully-stocked bars and a splurge of Cowshed toiletries. Size-wise, they run the Soho House gamut from Tiny to Extra Large. Private (and less private) outdoor space also varies from teeny garden-view verandas to huge first-floor terraces with tasselled hammocks and daybeds.

There are plenty of perks in every room: flip-flops, hooded cotton bathrobes, Shoreditch Grind espresso pods, and, of course, Roberts radios.
