How to Check the Value of Toys R Us Gift Card

If you have received a Toys R Us gift card, it’s important to know its value before making any purchases. Luckily, checking the value of your gift card is a simple and straightforward process. Here’s how you can check your Toys R Us gift card balance:

1. Visit the Toys R Us website
2. Locate the “Gift Cards” option on the menu bar and click on it
3. Select “Check Balance” from the drop-down list
4. Enter your gift card number and PIN (if applicable) in the fields provided
5. Click “Check Balance”

After completing these steps, you should see your gift card’s remaining balance displayed on the screen. You can also check your balance by calling Toys R Us Customer Service at 1-800-TOYSRUS (869-7787) or by visiting a Toys R Us store and speaking to an associate.

FAQs

How do I check the balance of my Toys R Us gift card online?

You can check your Toys R Us gift card balance online by visiting the Toys R Us website, selecting “Gift Cards” from the menu bar, and then choosing the “Check Balance” option. Enter your gift card number and PIN (if applicable) in the fields provided and click “Check Balance.” You will then see your gift card’s remaining balance displayed on the screen.

Can I check my Toys R Us gift card balance without a PIN number?

If your gift card does not have a PIN number, simply leave the field blank when checking your balance online or when speaking to a Toys R Us associate.

How do I check my Toys R Us gift card balance by phone?

To check your Toys R Us gift card balance by phone, call Toys R Us Customer Service at 1-800-TOYSRUS (869-7787). You will be prompted to enter your gift card number and PIN (if applicable) using your phone keypad. After providing this information, you will hear your gift card’s remaining balance.

Can I check my Toys R Us gift card balance in store?

Yes, you can check your Toys R Us gift card balance in store by speaking to an associate at the customer service desk or any register.

What should I do if my Toys R Us gift card is not working?

If your Toys R Us gift card is not working, double-check that you have entered the correct gift card number and PIN (if applicable) when trying to check its balance or use it to make a purchase. If the card still does not work, contact Toys R Us Customer Service at 1-800-TOYSRUS (869-7787) for assistance.

Can I use my Toys R Us gift card for online purchases?

Yes, you can use your Toys R Us gift card to make online purchases on the Toys R Us website.

Can I use my Toys R Us gift card to purchase items at Babies R Us?

Yes, you can use your Toys R Us gift card to purchase items at Babies R Us stores or on the Babies R Us website.

What happens if my Toys R Us gift card balance is less than the cost of my purchase?

If your Toys R Us gift card balance is less than the cost of your purchase, you will need to provide an additional form of payment to cover the remaining balance.

What should I do if my Toys R Us gift card is lost or stolen?

If your Toys R Us gift card is lost or stolen, contact Toys R Us Customer Service at 1-800-TOYSRUS (869-7787) as soon as possible to report the loss and request a replacement card.

Can I reload my Toys R Us gift card?

No, you cannot reload your Toys R Us gift card. Once the balance has been used, the card is no longer valid.

Does my Toys R Us gift card expire?

No, Toys R Us gift cards do not expire.

Can I return an item purchased with a Toys R Us gift card?

Yes, you can return an item purchased with a Toys R Us gift card for a refund. The refund will be issued in the form of a new gift card or on the original gift card used to make the purchase.

Can I transfer the balance of my Toys R Us gift card to another card?

No, you cannot transfer the balance of your Toys R Us gift card to another card.

Can I use my Toys R Us gift card to purchase other gift cards?

No, you cannot use your Toys R Us gift card to purchase other gift cards.
