Can mold cause brain fog?

Mold toxicity can manifest in different ways in people. It's more commonly linked to physical problems, such as difficulty breathing, fatigue, and headaches, but research shows that it can present itself in a psychiatric way, too. This includes brain fog, depression, anxiety, problems concentrating, and insomnia.

Can mold cause mental confusion?

Mold is also known to cause asthma and life-threatening primary and secondary infections in immune-compromised patients that have been exposed. What does mold brain fog feel like?

Brain Fog is a term my patients often use to describe the brain effects of mold toxicity. This term means that they experience loss of focus, loss of train of thought, difficulty with word finding, difficulty with problem solving, memory loss, and even confusion.

How do you detox from mold exposure?

Some proponents of mold detoxes claim activated charcoal will bind to mycotoxins, allowing you to detox after exposure to toxic mold. While doctors do use activated charcoal to treat cases of poisoning, no research currently backs up the claim that charcoal helps with the effects of mold exposure.

Do I have mold toxicity?

How do you get tested for mold exposure?

Blood test.
