10 Fun Things to Do in Redlands

Exploring Redlands’ Natural Beauty

Redlands, with its picturesque landscapes and abundant natural beauty, offers several outdoor activities for nature enthusiasts. One of the most popular attractions is the Redlands Municipal Airport Observation Deck, where visitors can watch planes take off and land while immersing themselves in the serene ambiance. Another must-visit spot is the San Timoteo Nature Sanctuary, which features scenic trails that showcase the region’s diverse flora and fauna. Hike along the paths and soak in the breathtaking views.

For a rejuvenating experience, head over to Prospect Park in Redlands and discover its tranquil settings. This charming park offers lush greenery, vibrant flowers, and even a duck pond. It’s the ideal place for picnics, strolls, or simply spending quality time with loved ones. In addition, the Crafton Hills Open Space Preserve offers opportunities for hiking, mountain biking, and horseback riding, where visitors can revel in Redlands’ natural playground.

Discovering Redlands’ Cultural Side

Redlands is a cultural hub with fascinating attractions that delve into the city’s history. The Kimberly Crest House & Gardens is a magnificent Victorian mansion that offers guided tours, showcasing its exquisite architecture, stunning gardens, and art exhibitions. Another historical gem is the Morey Mansion, a beautifully preserved 19th-century Victorian house that takes visitors back in time. Learn about its rich history and admire the intricate details that make it a captivating destination.

Art enthusiasts will enjoy exploring the Redlands Art Association Gallery, which presents a collection of diverse artworks created by local artists. The gallery hosts regular exhibitions, showcasing paintings, sculptures, ceramics, and other forms of artistic expression. For music lovers, the Redlands Bowl is the place to be. This outdoor amphitheater offers free summer concerts, exposing visitors to a variety of musical genres.

Engaging in Thrilling Adventures

For thrill-seekers, Redlands offers a range of adventurous activities to get your adrenaline pumping. Head to Escape Craft Brewery and challenge yourself with their escape rooms, where you’ll need to solve puzzles and unravel mysteries to escape within the time limit. Additionally, if you’re a fan of go-kart racing, SB Raceway will provide an exhilarating experience as you navigate the track at top speed, competing with friends and family.

To indulge in some friendly competition, pay a visit to All in Adventures, an interactive mystery game where you become the detective. Solve puzzles, gather clues, and uncover the truth in a thrilling race against the clock. For a unique outdoor adventure, try ziplining at the Flights of Fantasy Zipline, where you can glide through the breathtaking wilderness and enjoy an adrenaline-fueled excursion.

Unwinding and Dining

After all the excitement, it’s time to relax and enjoy some delicious food. Redlands offers a wide range of dining options to satisfy every palate. Head to Market Broiler for fresh seafood dishes or visit Rao’s Bakery for a delightful selection of pastries and cakes. If you’re in the mood for Mexican cuisine, make sure to stop by Don Orange Tacos for mouthwatering tacos and burritos.

For a laid-back evening, visit The State, a local craft beer establishment where you can enjoy a wide selection of beers on tap. If you’re a coffee lover, indulge in a cup of joe at Augie’s Coffee House, a cozy café known for its artisanal coffee brews. Finally, end your day at The Eating Room, a charming restaurant specializing in comfort food with a contemporary twist.

Conclusion

Whether you’re drawn to nature, culture, adventure, or simply enjoying good food, Redlands offers a variety of activities to suit all preferences. From exploring the city’s natural beauty to delving into its rich history and engaging in thrilling adventures, Redlands provides endless opportunities for fun and entertainment. So, plan your visit to this vibrant Californian city and embark on a memorable journey filled with excitement and discovery.
