Big Bank Hank (August 5, 1957 – November 11, 2014) was an old school rapper from the United States. He was a member of The Sugarhill Gang, the first hip hop group to score a hit song with “Rapper’s Delight” in 1979. He was also known as Imp the Dimp. Big Bank Hank passed away on November 11, 2014, from cancer.
