Lakers fans hound Ja Morant, Dillon Brooks in epic walk of shame after loss

The Memphis Grizzlies were eliminated by the Los Angeles Lakers in the first round of the 2023 NBA Playoffs on Friday. Both Ja Morant and Dillon Brooks walked from Crypto.com Arena to their hotel with a huge crowd surrounding them.

Brooks, 27, became public enemy number one by calling LeBron James "old" and fans reminded him of his shortcomings.

Following the Game 6 loss, Brooks reportedly left the Grizzlies locker room before media arrived, without even showering.

Brooks ignored the Purple & Gold fans yelling at him as he slowly made his way down the street.

Morant, 23, on the other hand, was happy to play along with the crowd as they sang the goodbye song.

Ja Morant's walk of shame

One fan even had the audacity to ask Morant what his favorite song is to play at a strip club.

Morant obliged by answering with one of Rich Homie Quan's tracks, making it quite clear that he's unphased with the elimination.

Brooks and Morant scored 10 points each in Game 6 on a combined 7-for-27 shooting from the floor.

Morant's "I'm fine in the West" comments are now coming back to haunt him, but he doesn't seem to mind.
