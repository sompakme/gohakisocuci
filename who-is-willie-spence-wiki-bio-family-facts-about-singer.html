Who is Willie Spence? Wiki, Bio, Family &amp; Facts About Singer

Willie Spence is a well-known American singer, TV show personality, media face, YouTuber, lyricist, Internet star, and social media influencer, who was born on June 18, 1999. He rose to fame for his appearance on the American Idol season 19. He is one of the top three finalists of the show. 

Spence has also released his singles on his official YouTube channel. His cover song ‘Diamond’ has the highest views of his account. Moreover, he has also featured in other live shows with famous singers. 

Biography

Willie was born on 18 June 1999 in West Palm Beach, located in Florida, United States. He is currently living in Douglas, Georgia with his family.

He shifted to Georgia when he was in second grade. He took admission to Coffee High School for his higher education. Later, he enrolled himself in Florida State University for his further studies.

Career

Willie was just three years old when he gets attracted to singing. His mother and grandfather have always supported him to continue his career in singing. He has participated in the popular singing show of America named ‘American Idol 19’. He also posts many singing videos on his self-titled YouTube channel.

American Idol 19

Spencer gave auditions for American Idol at San Diego, California. He sang the song of Rihanna titled ‘Diamonds’. All the judges got impressed by his dynamic voice and smooth vocal. He got selected for the American Idol and now he is in the top 3 competitors of the show. 

When only 4 contestants were left, he sang the song Glory by John Legend and a duet song named ‘What They’ll Say About Us’ with Grace Kinstler. Willie got so much love from his fans and enter the finale of the show. In this round, Casey Bishop was eliminated from the American idol. 

The show is about to reach its finale week on upcoming Sunday (May 23, 2021) and the country will found the winner of American Idol Season 19, The show will air on ABC’s channel at 8:00 pm. The top three contestants are Willie Spencer, Chayce Beckham, and Grace Kinstler.

Famous Ocean and Luh Kel have featured in the music video of Spencer. Apart from this, he has also sung many songs such as Chance on me, Reckless, One Minute with God, Forever More, Champion, and Notice Me.
