Watch Voldemort Sing Uptown Funk in Harry Potter Parody

You can’t say his name but you can sing along with him to this “Uptown Funk” Harry Potter parody video. Lord Voldemort sings “Dark Lord Funk” in this new video made by Brigham Young University students.

Directed and written by Keith Allen, the video is full of Harry Potter Easter eggs for fans of the book and movie series. Allen, who plays Harry Potter in the video, has taken a break from his graduate studies to create parody videos for his YouTube channel KFaceTV. Watch and sing along with the lyrics, posted below.
