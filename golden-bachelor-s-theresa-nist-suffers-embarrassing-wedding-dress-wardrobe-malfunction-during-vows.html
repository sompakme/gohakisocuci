Golden Bachelor's Theresa Nist suffers 'embarrassing' wedding dress wardrobe malfunction during vows

GOLDEN Bachelor star Theresa Nist has had an unfortunate wardrobe malfunction during her wedding.

The Golden Bachelor Gerry Turner and his new wife Theresa tied the knot during a live episode of the show on Thursday night.

The bride looked gorgeous in a pure white mermaid dress covered in a beautiful pattern.

The dress sparkled under all of the lights from the ceremony and the cameras.

She wore a long white veil over her dress that covered her arms and the entire back of her dress.

Still, it was see-through enough to view her sweetheart neckline and the off-the-shoulder straps on both her arms.
