TXT: Profile, Members, Ages, Birthdays, Positions

1. Soobin

  • Stage Name: Soobin
  • Full Name: Choi Soo Bin
  • Korean Name: 최수빈
  • Birth Place: Korea
  • Birthday: Dec 5, 2000
  • Height: 185 cm (6'1")
  • Weight: 67 kg (148 lbs)
  • Blood Type: A
  • Zodiac: Sagittarius

Soobin is the leader of TXT. Nicknamed cucumber because of his tall height, Soobin is the tallest member in TXT standing at 6’1” or 185cm. His hobbies include listening to music and reading (specially life-helping books). Soobin’s favorite animal is the raccoon. His favorite colors are sky blue and yellow.

  • Birthday: Sep 13, 1999

5. Huening Kai

  • Stage Name: Huening Kai
  • Full Name: Kai Kamal Huening
  • Korean Name: 휴닝카이
  • Birth Place: United States
  • Birthday: Aug 14, 2002
  • Height: 183 cm (6'0")

Huening Kai can speak Mandarin, Korean, English, and Portuguese. He has the “golden touch” – he can play the drums, guitar, and the piano.
