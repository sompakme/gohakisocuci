WWE NXT Great American Bash Full Card &amp; Start Time

WWE will hold the next NXT Great American Bash on Sunday, July 30th, 2023 at the H-E-B Center at Cedar Park in Cedar Park, Texas. The card already had a few big matches, but the card wasn’t finished, as they added even more action this week on NXT. The event will start at 8:00 PM, with a 30-minute kick-off show going down at 7:30 PM.

Gable Steveson made his big decision on WWE NXT this week. That segment ended with Steveson saying that he will face Baron Corbin at the Great American Bash. Interestingly enough, Baron Corbin will wrestle an Olympic Gold Medalist in his first match with Steveson, and he also wrestled another Olympic Gold Medalist, Kurt Angle,in his final match.

Ilja Dragunov will also challenge Carmelo Hayes for the NXT Title. Also, Tiffany Stratton will put her NXT Women’s Title on the line against Thea Hale in a submission match.

Tony D’Angelo and Stacks will finally get an NXT Tag Team Title shot against Gallus. That storyline with The Family has been building for months.

NXT also added a kickoff show match to the event during this week’s show. Dragon Lee, Nathan Frazer, Yulisa León, and Valentina Feroz vs. The Meta-Four will take up the kickoff show match this time.

All that and more is slated for the Great American Bash this Sunday. Keep checking back with us here at Ringside News for more on this story, and so much more. You may also check out the updated card below.
