The Reason Queen Elizabeth Wants Camilla to Be Queen Camilla

Over the weekend, Queen Elizabeth issued a royal proclamation — well, it wasn't that serious, but she did share some news — saying that when Prince Charles ascends to the throne, she wants Camilla Parker-Bowles to be known as queen consort, much like Prince Philip was prince consort during her reign. Royal historian Robert Lacey told People that the decision wasn't taken lightly, but drew upon feelings that came up with Philip's death and her own parents.

"In the message, she was acknowledging her own mortality and looking to the future," he said. "As she thought about her father George VI, she would have also thought about her mother and the importance she played as a partner to him. And then, of course, with the passing of Prince Philip, she is considering the sacrifices and difficulties of being a consort."

Royal fans have been noticing hints of the news for years now. In 2016, the queen announced that Camilla would be a part of the Privy Council. Then, in 2021, Camilla became a member of the Order of the Garter, which People describes as "the most senior order of knighthood in the British honors system."

"This was the big remaining question mark and it has now been resolved, and any consent from the Prime Minister of the day for the title is a formality," Lacey added. "Some people might have thought she would look backwards over her reign with this message, but she was in fact looking forwards in a positive way."

Back in 2005, when Charles married Camilla, many thought that she would take the title of princess consort when Charles became king. With the weekend's news, Elizabeth guarantees that Camilla will be crowned alongside Charles during his coronation.

After the queen's announcement, Charles released a statement that acknowledged the importance of the decision.

"We are deeply conscious of the honour represented by my mother's wish. As we have sought together to serve and support Her Majesty and the people of our communities, my darling wife has been my own steadfast support throughout," it read.
