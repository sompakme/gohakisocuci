Abide by Dwell Songs | MultiTracks.com

Dwell Songs is a community of worship leaders and musicians based in Little Rock who are passionate about seeing spiritual renewal and awakening in this generation. Led by Aaron Williams, Dwell Songs is part of the Dwell movement, a broader discipleship and worship movement that equips and empowers young leaders through events, resources, and community. 

Their latest single "Abide" declares who God is and voices our necessary dependence on Him always. "You're the Way the Truth and the Life. You're the well that never runs dry. I'm the branch and You are the Vine. Draw me close and teach me to abide."
