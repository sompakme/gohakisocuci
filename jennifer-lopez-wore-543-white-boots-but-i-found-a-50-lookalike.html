Jennifer Lopez Wore $543 White Boots, but I Found a $50 Lookalike

When I think of winter, colors like cool pink, cozy cream, and icy blue come to mind. But I’d be doing you a disservice if I didn’t call out classic winter white. The crisp, neutral shade is reminiscent of frosty sidewalks, falling snowflakes, and brisk temperatures, making it one of the season’s leading hues. 

Celebrities like Anne Hathaway, Meghan Markle, and Selena Gomez are in full agreement, wearing the snowy shade in different forms, from blazers to turtlenecks. But if going for a full-blown white outfit is a bit too much for you, then consider taking notes from Jennifer Lopez, who donned the winter-perfect hue in the most practical way.  

Shutz Maryana Pointed-Toe Boot 

Lopez recently wore $543 white stiletto boots from Paris Texas. The knee-high boots featured a pointed toe, tall stiletto heel, and croc-embossed leather. Unfortunately, the gorgeous shoes have since sold out, but various brands offer lookalikes, like this Western-inspired Schutz pair featuring a 3.5-inch heel height, soaring shaft, and pull-on style.

Modatope Knee-High Faux Croc Stiletto Boot

Modatope offers an even more identical pair that includes the same knee-grazing height, sharp toe, and side zipper, which makes this shoe easy to put on and take off. This boot style is versatile, too; one shopper was able to wear it to both a ’60s-themed party and work, while another reviewer claimed the footwear is “sexy” and “comfortable.”

Frankie Croc-Embossed Knee-High Boot

If the pencil-thin heel isn't for you (I get it), consider the Frankie Boot from Chinese Laundry, which is 41 percent off right now. The textured shoe possesses the same great qualities as J.Lo’s pair, but includes a thick wooden heel that slopes at the top and provides stability.    

No matter which brand you secure your white J.Lo-inspired boots from, you’ll have an easy footwear option that never clashes with anything you pair them with. Consider styling them with a two-piece skirt set like Lopez, pull them over a pair of skinny jeans, or coordinate with a matching, crescent-shaped purse.
