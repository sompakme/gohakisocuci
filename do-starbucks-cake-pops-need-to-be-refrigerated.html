Do Starbucks cake pops need to be refrigerated?

Answer

Cake pops, like the majority of cakes, do not need refrigeration. Cake pops should be refrigerated if they are prepared with whipped cream, cream cheese, or pastry cream. If they are created with any of these ingredients, they should be refrigerated as well to prevent them from rotting.

Keep your cake pops at room temperature for up to a week before serving them. In order to keep your cake pops for up to two weeks or up to one month, put them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. To keep your cake pops for up to three months, pack them in big plastic freezer bags and store them in your freezer.
