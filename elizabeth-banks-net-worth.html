Early Life and Education

Elizabeth Banks was born in Pittsfield, Massachusetts, on February 10, 1974. She is the daughter of Ann Mitchell and Mark P. Mitchell, who worked as a factory worker. Banks was raised in a tight-knit family and attended Pittsfield High School. Later, she went on to study at the University of Pennsylvania, where she earned a degree in communications.

Career

Elizabeth Banks began her career as an actress in the early 2000s, appearing in films like “Shaft” and “Spider-Man.” She became a household name after playing the role of Effie Trinket in “The Hunger Games” films. Banks has also made a name for herself in Hollywood as a director and producer, with films like “Pitch Perfect 2” and “Charlie’s Angels” to her name.

Personal Life

Elizabeth Banks married Max Handelman in 2003. The couple met at the University of Pennsylvania and have two children together. Banks is known for her philanthropic work and has been a strong advocate for Planned Parenthood.

Real Estate

Legacy
