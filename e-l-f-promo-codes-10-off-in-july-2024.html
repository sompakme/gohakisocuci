With orders of $35 or more, you can get free US standard shipping at Elf. This shipping usually costs $6.50 for all other orders. Your order should arrive within 2-6 working days. Check this page for free shipping codes and the best Elf promo codes for 2024.

Shop trending makeup for less with the Sale page on the Elf Cosmetics website. You can shop all year round with regular savings of up to 20% off selected products. Start shopping Elf Cosmetics for less with a first-purchase discount. When you sign up for the email newsletter, you will be sent an Elf coupon code for $5 off your first $20 purchase. How can I get free rewards from Elf Cosmetics?

You can get two free miniature beauty products at the checkout when your order total is $35 or more. You'll also get an additional gift if you spend over $50. What is the returns policy at Elf?

Elf accepts any returns that might be damaged with shipping. However, they cannot accept returns for most products. You have up to 30 days to return products such as selected concealer and foundation, as listed on their returns page. Check the return page on the Elf website for a list of accepted return products.
