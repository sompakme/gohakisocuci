Alexa Bliss Hints At Going After NXT Womens Title

Alexa Bliss competed against Charlotte Flair at Extreme Rules last year. However, that match didn’t end the way Bliss would have liked as she lost in quick fashion against The Queen. It seems she hinted at going after the NXT Women’s Championship as well.

After a lot of anticipation, Alexa Bliss finally made her return earlier last month. After undergoing several therapy sessions, Alexa Bliss was deemed to be cured as well.

Bliss competed in the Women’s Elimination Chamber match on February 19th and had an impressive showing, despite not being able to win the match. Since then she has not been seen on WWE television.

While speaking during the recent WWE 2K22 launch stream, Alexa Bliss hinted at going to NXT to challenge Mandy Rose for the NXT Women’s Championship.

“NXT! I’ve never won the NXT Women’s Championship. You never know. You never know.” 

Alexa Bliss is certainly no stranger to NXT as she spent the first three years of her WWE career there before being called up to the main roster in 2016. We will have to wait and see whether bliss will return to NXT and feud with Mandy Rose for the NXT Women’s Championship or not.
