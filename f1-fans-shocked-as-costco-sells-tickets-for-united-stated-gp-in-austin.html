F1 fans shocked as Costco sells tickets for United Stated GP in Austin

Costco is now offering Formula 1 fans the chance to purchase tickets for the United States Grand Prix.

The well-known supermarket chain has introduced a three-day pass for the event, which is scheduled to take place at the Circuit of the Americas (COTA) from October 20 to 22.

This move has caused a stir due to its novelty within the top tier of motor racing.

This unexpected development has raised questions about the ticket sales situation.

Formula 1 fans are left wondering if ticket demand has dipped, considering that acquiring a ticket for this event was previously quite challenging.

Moreover, the competition has intensified with the emergence of other venues such as Miami and Las Vegas.

The Costco tickets for the Grand Prix in Austin, priced at $350, offer a straightforward method of purchase-directly at the supermarket.

The pass encompasses a three-day access to the Formula 1 United States Grand Prix, granting general admission to any of the designated areas within the Circuit of the Americas (COTA).

Each Costco membership allows the acquisition of up to five tickets, as specified on the supermarket's website.

Here are the key features of the pass

  • A three-day pass for the Formula 1 United States Grand Prix.
  • General admission access.
  • Wristband collection at the Grand Plaza box office.

How to redeem your voucher

  • To redeem your U.S. GP voucher at Costco, follow these steps:
  • You will receive an eVoucher code via email from letsgo@fevo.com.
  • Click on the provided link within the email and input your code in the designated field.
  • If you possess multiple codes, you can add each one in succession.
  • Proceed to your cart and continue.
  • Enter your contact details and confirm, omitting credit card information.
  • Verify your phone number and email address.
