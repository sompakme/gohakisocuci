Leagues Cup 2023: Carlos Salcedo calls for the use of VAR in the Leagues Cup after foul on Messi in

After falling 2-1 to Inter Miami on Friday night in the Leagues Cup, Cruz Azul defender Carlos Salcedo made his anger known on social media.

The Mexican defender wasn't pleased with referee's work in a match that saw the MLS side come out on top thanks to a last-minute goal by Lionel Messi via a freekick.

According to Salcedo, the foul that allowed the Argentine to score shouldn't have been given and he didn't hold back in criticizing the work of Said Martinez, who obviously felt Kevin Castano took down Messi.

Salcedo was so incensed that he asked the Leagues Cup organizers to have referees use VAR more freely in order to avoid mistakes during matches.

"Please Leagues Cup make the referees use VAR.... We are all human beings and we make mistakes, but where is the foul that the referee says exists on the part of my teammate Kevin (Castano). Let's do things the way they should be," he wrote.

The defender accompanied his tweet with a video, in which you can see the play in which the referee says Messi was fouled.

Salcedo was also part of he play, as he was the player who prevented the ball from reaching the Argentine.

However, the referee saw that Castano fouled Messi from behind, so he awarded the free kick, which allowed Tata Martino's men to claim a much needed victory.
