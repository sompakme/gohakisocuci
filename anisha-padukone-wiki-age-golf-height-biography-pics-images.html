Introduction :

Deepika Padukone is widely famous in Bollywood, but her beautiful, talented sister Anisha Padukone, isn’t to be forgotten about. She herself is a professional golfer who consistently stays within the top 10 rankings in India.

Personal Life : Parents, Family and Education

  • Anisha was born on February 2, 1991, to parents – Father Prakash Padukone & Mother Ujjala Padukone.
  • Her father happens to be one of the best Badminton players to have ever come from India, while her older sister is a Bollywood superstar.
  • Needless to say, she was born into a famous last name and bloodline.
  • Education : Anisha attended Mount Carmel College in Banaglore, India.

Love for Sports :

  • At only 10 years old, Anisha vowed to take on sports at a professional level someday.
  • This decision led her to change schools to a more sports focused-one.
  • Being a natural athlete, Anisha ended up playing basketball at the State level, and cricket at the national level (before committing to golf completely)

Relationships :

  • Anisha and her only sister Deepika remain close, even though they rarely get to see one another with their individual schedules.
  • Deepika is 5 years older than Anisha, giving her the relationship like a Second Mom in terms of care and protection.
  • Anisha is always brutally honest when she watches & reviews Deepika’s films with her.
  • Her favourite Deepika’s films are : Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Love Aaj Kal, Cocktail and Karthik Calling Karthik
  • At the beginning of Deepika’s career launch, Anisha wasn’t quite sure how she felt about all the starlet attention.
  • She has since gotten used to it, but still likes to keep a certain amount of life private.
  • As far as romantic relationships go, Anisha is fairly career focused right now, and has remained private in this area of her life.

Anisha Padukone Golf Career :

  • Anisha’s father’s success in Badminton is ultimately what inspired her to take up a career in golf.
  • Having emerged herself in golf at the international level, she is well representing India for the sport.
  • She vows to continue her focus on personal development for the sport.
