Nigerian Filmmaker Kunle Afolayan Confirms Wizkid Set for Acting Debut, Video Warms Hearts

  • Renowned Nigerian filmmaker Kunle Afolayan officially verified the speculations surrounding Wizkid venturing into Nollywood
  • The revelation slightly surfaced when the Made in Lagos maestro playfully hinted at his intention to step into the movie scene
  • Shortly after that, the film producer disclosed that there was indeed a collaborative project underway between him and the music icon

Nigerian filmmaker Kunle Afolayan has confirmed rumours about Afrobeats singer Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun making his debut in Nollywood.

Legit.ng earlier reported that the Made in Lagos hitmaker jokingly gave his fans a hint of his plans to make an appearance in a movie scene.

A few minutes after making the broadcast on his Instagram story, the award-winning movie producer took to his account to announce that "something was brewing" between him and the singer.
