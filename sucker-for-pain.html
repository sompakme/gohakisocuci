Sucker For Pain

The highly anticipated "Suicide Squad" movie also has a pretty anticipated soundtrack to go along with it-- we recently got a look at the tracklist, which is stacked with appearances from Lil Wayne, Kevin Gates, Action Bronson, Kehlani, Eminem, Grimes, Rick Ross and many more. Today we're getting to hear one of the biggest collaborations off the soundtrack (I mean 'biggest' in terms of the number of artists on the song)-- "Sucker for Pain" featuring Lil Wayne, Wiz Khalifa, Logic, Ty Dolla $ign, X Ambassadors and Imagine Dragon. A line-up like that could either be a recipe for a disaster or something extremely dope, and it happens to be the latter. The music video for the song is expected to drop later tonight, but ahead of the visuals we're getting the official audio stream.

It's an organ key-laced beat with creeping guitar strings and a wavy (/catchy) chorus. Suicide Squad: The Album drops on August 5th, as does the movie. You can pre-order the album on iTunes below (and get this song instantly).

Who has the best verse here? Weezy F went offffff. Case in point, Quotable Lyrics.
