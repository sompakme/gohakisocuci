Victor Wembanyama's sister Eve gets mistaken for the player himself with epic Halloween costume

'Wemby' fever has swept through San Antonio this season. The 19-year-old rookie has been a major hit, making an excellent start to life in the NBA- this week he scored a season high of 38 points as the Spurs defeated the Phoenix Suns on Thursday.

The Frenchman's sister Eve decided to celebrate her brother's achievements in style on Tuesday, dressing up as the player himself for Halloween. The outfit has taken the internet by storm, with many labelling it this year's Halloween costume of the year.

The costume was scarily accurate- dressed in Wemby's jersey and with his characteristic mustache painted on her face with some makeup, Eve could have easily been mistaken for the Spurs star. She even slicked her hair back to mimic her brother's cut.

Fans couldn't get enough of the costume and took to social media to express their delight at the epic Halloween attempt. "Thought that was Wemby at first," wrote one user on X. "Costume of the year," wrote another.
