Tunebat

Tunebat is designed for DJs, producers, and music enthusiasts to allow them to find data of a huge number of songs.
You can discover tracks for harmonic mixing and any other purpose.
The platform offers a key and BPM finder, and will soon add a vocal remover and song mastering feature.
