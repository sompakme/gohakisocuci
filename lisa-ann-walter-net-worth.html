Lisa Ann Walter Net Worth

Lisa Ann Walter is an American stand-up comedian and actress born on August 3, 1963 in Silver Spring, MD. She is best known for creating and starring in her own sitcom and for her guest appearances in several films. She has also served as a judge on the reality show The Next Best Thing: Who Is the Greatest Celebrity Impersonator?

About

Stand-up Comedian who went into acting by creating and starring in her own sitcom and guest starred in several films. She later served as a judge on The Next Best Thing: Who Is the Greatest Celebrity Impersonator?

Before Fame

After several years of pure standup comedy, she developed the sitcom Life's Work for ABC, which she also starred in.  

Trivia

She starred in Bruce Almighty and the 1998 remake The Parent Trap. 

Family Life

She was married to Sam Baum, a part-time actor, and the couple had three sons and one daughter. 

Associated With

In the late 2000s, she turned to reality TV, creating the Oxygen competition show Dance Your Ass Off, hosted by Melanie Brown.
