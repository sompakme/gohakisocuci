Val Venis Shares Controversial Take After Bray Wyatts Passing

Bray Wyatt’s passing has caused many to speak out in tribute. Val Venis on the other hand, had his own takeaway from this tragic story.

Val Venis heard this report about Bray Wyatt’s passing, and he went on an unhinged rant after he read Sean Sapp reveal Windham Rotunda’s cause of death.

As the Fightful reporter noted on the day that Bray Wyatt’s passing broke, “Windham Rotunda (Bray Wyatt) got COVID that exacerbated a heart issues. There was a lot of positive progress towards a return and his recovery. Unfortunately, today he suffered a heart attack and passed away.” Sean Sapp also made sure to note that, “I was given permission to reveal” the information.

Val Venis saw this tweet, and he went off. The former WWE Superstar posted on X to say, “100% COMPLETELY IRRESPONSIBLE REPORTING BY THIS LOSER @SeanRossSapp! HORRIBLE REPORTING DRIVING MULTIPLE COMMENTS/QUOTES SUGGESTING PEOPLE GET THE CLOT SHOT! EVIL!”

Val Venis included a couple of screenshots with this tweet that had a few controversial takes to support his view. We will not be embedding that post in this article.

That post from Val Venis was not all. He also dropped a post later on that said, “I am only concerned with his reporting. That vaccine is dangerous. To watch a human being push others to get the vax is something I will always stand against.”

Val Venis did not stop there. His right-wing views are seen as extreme by some, and he has been quite vocal about his anti-vaxxing views. This also got him in some trouble on social media, as his account has been suspended in the past.

“Lots of delusional @SeanRossSapp rats (gender neutral term) talking smack. Pretty sure most of them are fully vaxxed & boosted. You would find plenty of pronouns in their bios. No more needs to be said. If you read this, please say a prayer for these leftist delusionals.”

The Big Valbowski also posted a bit later to carry on his narrative. He is not backing down, either, as he continues speaking about his views against the COVID-19 vaccine.

Val Venis also noted on X to say that Bray Wyatt was a creative genius. His complaint had nothing to do with Bray Wyatt, but the fact that his story could be used to convince people to get a vaccine.
