Katie Couric Bio, Age, Husband, Siblings

Katie Couric was born on January 7 1957 in Arlington.


Katie Couric is a product of the University of Virginia where she had her Degree in English.


Katie Couric is a New York Times Bestselling Author and also a Television personality.


She hosted NBC Today before she became a news anchor at CBS and also moved to ABC where she hosted some programmes.

She recently disclosed that she was diagnosed with breast cancer.

Katie Couric Age

In 2022 Katie Couric is 65 years old.

Katie Couric Husband

Katie got married to the love of her life on January 24, 1989. Her husband is called Jay Monahan and she had two children. However, he passed away, unfortunately. After his demise, she married John Molner on June 21, 2014.

Katie Couric Siblings

Katie Couric had a sister called Emily. However, Emily died of pancreatic cancer when she was aged 54.
