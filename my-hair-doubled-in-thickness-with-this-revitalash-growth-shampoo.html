My Hair Doubled in Thickness With This Revitalash Growth Shampoo

Around the middle of 2020, I began to lose my hair. My once unmanageable, thick, long locks began breaking off at the root, and even more alarmingly, shedding in clusters without signs of regrowth. Like for many others, pandemic-related stress was likely the main culprit of my thinning scalp (though attempting to bleach my hair with the confidence and skill of a five-year-old probably didn't help, either).

In the following months, I chose to be willfully ignorant about the issue while getting creative with different hairstyles, until Revitalash Cosmetics — the brand famous for its Meghan Markle-used lash growth serum — offered me a sample of its thickening shampoo. Already a fan of the brand's shopper-adored brow growth product, I agreed to give it a go with the hopes that it would bring my strands back to life. Fortunately enough, it did, and now I'm stocking up while it's on sale for 20 percent off on Dermastore using the code REFRESH. But hurry, the sale ends March 10.

Revitalash Thickening Shampoo

Made from a special blend of lipids, biotin, and green tea extracts (which the brand uses in its brow and lash serums, too), the Revitalash Cosmetics Thickening Shampoo repairs and strengthens broken and brittle strands while preserving length and fullness. Thanks to nourishing additions like flax protein and jojoba oil, the shampoo also gently removes dandruff, oil, and product buildup from the scalp without stripping away moisture.

I incorporated the shampoo, along with the accompanying Thickening Conditioner, into my shower routine three times a week. Following the directions, I massaged a dime-size amount into my scalp and waited for about 10 minutes before rinsing it out. The short-term result? Immediately bouncier, brighter waves that looked freshly blown-out (even though I don't actually own a blow dryer).

After about a month of consistent use, I noticed that the thinning patches around my temples had sprouted soft baby hairs when I was putting my hair up into a ponytail. Two months later, it was as if I'd never lost that hair at all. Now that I'm through my second bottle, it's clear that Revitalash Cosmetics' shampoo is responsible for doubling my mane's thickness — so, of course, I'm recommending it to just about anyone who'll listen.

If you're interested in getting in on the magic yourself, grab a bottle while it's on sale for $30 on Dermstore using the code REFRESH.
