How many romances can Geralt have?

Geralt has two main romance options in The Witcher 3: Yennefer and Triss. Ultimately, only one of these sorceresses can be Geralt's partner by the time that you finish the game and its expansions.

Can Geralt romance multiple people?

Geralt can romance them all with no repercussions on the others — this even pertains to his long-term goals with Triss and Yennefer.

What happens if you romance more than one person in The Witcher 3?

If you attempt to equally romance both Triss and Yennefer throughout the game you will lose both. Later in the game, after helping Triss and Yennefer in Novigrad before the search for the Sunstone they will attempt to seduce Geralt together, suggesting he can have both of them.
