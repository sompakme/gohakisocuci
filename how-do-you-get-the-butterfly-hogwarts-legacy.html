How do you get the butterfly Hogwarts Legacy?

Hogsmeade Valley Butterfly Location Players can find a group of butterflies in Hogsmeade Valley. Travel to the East Hogsmeade Valley Floo Flame and head to the opposite cliffs to the south. The butterflies swarm and fly to a chest beside the nearby Merlin Trial.

How do I get the butterflies in Hogwarts Legacy?

You can find a group of butterflies on the higher ridgeline above the Quidditch Pitch in the South Hogwarts Region. From the pitch, look to a high trail up in the southwest passing a large tree grove, and the butterflies will appear right in front of a large natural stone archway.

Why can’t i find the butterflies in Hogwarts Legacy?

Butterfly chests can be found all across the open world of Hogwarts Legacy, but you won’t see them on the in-game map to start with. You’ll need to head to the general region where butterflies have been spotted by other players, and look around with as much patience as you can muster.

Where is the butterfly in the forbidden forest Hogwarts Legacy?

To begin the quest, you will need to head over to the Forbidden Forest by setting a destination point on their map. Once inside the forest, the destination point can be followed to a bridge within the woods, where an additional marker will appear on the map as a small butterfly.

Where is the moth in Hogsmeade?

Move behind the buildings going North to go around the back of the Hog’s Head Inn by the river. Look for a large cask on the docks, and look around the corner for the wayward moth. Use Lumos to attract the moth to your wand, then guide it back to the frame to claim a Field Guide Page.

How do you get the third butterfly in witches tomb?

All three moths are missing from this door, so you’ll need to collect them and bring them back. Two are on either side of this circular room, and the third is behind a set of broken doors, just to the right of the moth door, that you’ll need to cast Depulso on to blast open.

Where are the butterflies in Manor Cape?

Turn 180 degrees to face the Merlin altar and you’ll notice some black bramble. Activate Lumos to walk past the bramble and you’ll find the butterflies. Escort them up to the final stone housing to complete this Merlin Trial.
